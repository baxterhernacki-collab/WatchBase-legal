Common questions

I forgot my password — how do I reset it?

On the sign-in screen, tap "Forgot password?", enter your email, and a reset link will be sent to you. Tapping that link opens the app to a screen where you can set a new password.

How do I permanently delete a watch?

Open the watch's detail screen and tap "Delete Permanently." This is different from "Remove," which just archives the watch — permanent delete removes the watch, its photo, and all wear, service, and accuracy history, and cannot be undone.

How do I delete my entire account?

From the dashboard screen, tap "Delete Account," type your email to confirm, and submit. This permanently deletes your account and all data associated with it, and cannot be undone.

How do I restore a purchase I already made?

Open the Pro paywall screen and use the "Restore Purchases" option, which re-links your existing App Store/Play Store subscription to your account.

Is my data private?

Yes — your collection is only visible to your own account. See our Privacy Policy for full details on what's collected and how it's stored.
