Watch Space — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Watch Space ("the app") is a personal watch-collection tracker. This policy explains what information the app collects, why, and how you can delete it. Watch Space is built for individual collectors and does not run ads, sell data, or share your information with data brokers.

Information we collect

DataWhy we collect it
Email address and password Used to create and sign in to your account. Passwords are never visible to us — they are handled by our authentication provider, Supabase, using industry-standard hashing.
Watch collection details Brand, model, purchase price, estimated value, service history, wear history, accuracy logs, insurance details, and photos you add — this is the core data the app exists to store for you.
Photos taken with the in-app scanner When you use the "Scan" feature to identify a watch, the photo is sent to Anthropic's Claude AI API for one-time identification (brand, model, estimated value, etc.). Anthropic processes this photo to generate the identification result but Watch Space does not use it to train any model. If you save the watch to your collection, the photo is then stored in our private, access-controlled Supabase storage tied to your account.
Face ID / Touch ID / device biometrics If you turn on the optional app-lock setting, your device's biometric authentication is used to unlock the app. This happens entirely on your device via Apple's/Google's own biometric APIs — Watch Space never receives, stores, or transmits any biometric data.
Purchase and subscription status If you subscribe to Watch Space Pro, purchase and entitlement status is managed by RevenueCat and the App Store/Play Store. We receive your entitlement status (e.g. "Pro" or "Free") but not your payment details — those are handled entirely by Apple/Google.

Watch Space does not collect your location, contacts, browsing history, or any advertising identifiers, and does not use third-party analytics or ad-tracking SDKs.

Where your data is stored

  • Supabase — our database and file-storage provider, hosts your account data and photos. Access is restricted to your own account via row-level security; no other user or the general public can read your data.
  • Anthropic — processes photos you submit to the Scan feature for one-time AI identification.
  • RevenueCat / Apple / Google — manage subscription purchases and entitlement status.

Your controls

  • Delete a single watch — permanently deletes that watch's record, photo, and all related wear/service/accuracy history. Available from any watch's detail screen ("Delete Permanently").
  • Delete your account — permanently deletes your entire account: every watch, photo, and log tied to it, and your login credentials. Available from the dashboard screen ("Delete Account"). This action is immediate and cannot be undone.
  • Hide values — an in-app setting to mask dollar amounts on-screen.
  • App lock — an optional setting requiring Face ID/Touch ID/device PIN to open the app, including automatically re-locking after the app has been backgrounded.

Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account exists. If you delete a single watch or your entire account through the in-app controls above, the corresponding data is deleted immediately from our systems — we do not keep a recoverable backup of deleted data beyond routine short-term database backups used for disaster recovery, which age out on their own schedule.

Children's privacy

Watch Space is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change accordingly. Material changes will be communicated in-app where practical.
